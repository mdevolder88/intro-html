Recipe for Five Bird Roast

Ingredients

  • 2 large duck breasts
  • 2 large duck legs
  • 2 large chicken breasts
  • 4 grouse breasts
  • 6 partridge breasts
  • 300 gram of sausage meat
  • 100 gram of semidried cranberries
  • 100 grams of chopped pistachio nuts
  • 1 teaspoon of crushed juniper berries
  • 20 slices or Parma ham

When you gathered everything for this recipe, remember A Bird in the Hand is Worth Two in the Bush

Method

  1. Debone all the birds. Cut the meat into thin strips.
  2. Mix all the ingedrients (except the Parmaham) in a large bowl until it becomes a mass.
  3. Roll the whole thing in cling foil and form into a log. Remove cling foil and wrap in Parma ham
  4. Apply new cling film, roll thigthly shut and place in a pan with hot water. The water should be kept at 75 degrees Celcius. Let it cook for 2 and a half hours.
  5. Remove from pan and remove cling foil. Place in a preheated 160 degree Celcius oven for 2 hours.
  6. Place log on a serving plate and let rest for 20 minutes, then carve up and serve.
