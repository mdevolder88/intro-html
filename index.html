Recipe for Five Bird Roast

Ingredients

    2 large duck breasts 2 large duck legs 2 large chicken breasts 4 grouse breasts 6 partridge breasts 300 gram of sausage meat 100 gram of semidried cranberries 100 grams of chopped pistachio nuts 1 teaspoon of crushed juniper berries 20 slices or Parma ham

When you gathered everything for this recipe, remember A Bird in the Hand is Worth Two in the Bush

Method

    Debone all the birds. Cut the meat into thin strips. Mix all the ingedrients (except the Parmaham) in a large bowl until it becomes a mass. Roll the whole thing in cling foil and form into a log. Remove cling foil and wrap in Parma ham Apply new cling film, roll thigthly shut and place in a pan with hot water. The water should be kept at 75 degrees Celcius. Let it cook for 2 and a half hours. Remove from pan and remove cling foil. Place in a preheated 160 degree Celcius oven for 2 hours.
